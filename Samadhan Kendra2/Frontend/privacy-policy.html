Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how Samadhan Kendra collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use Samadhan Kendra, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, city, and profile picture
  • Issue Reports: Photos, videos, descriptions, and location data (GPS coordinates)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our platform, features used, and preferences
  • Communication: Messages sent to support, feedback, and survey responses

Location Information

We collect location data to:

  • Pinpoint the exact location of reported civic issues
  • Route issues to the correct municipal departments
  • Provide location-based services and notifications
  • Analyze issue patterns in specific areas

Note: You can control location permissions through your device settings.

Media Content

Photos and videos you upload help us:

  • Document and verify reported issues
  • Provide evidence to municipal authorities
  • Track issue resolution progress
  • Improve our AI detection systems

How We Use Your Information

Primary Purposes

  • Issue Resolution: Process and route civic issues to appropriate authorities
  • Service Improvement: Enhance platform functionality and user experience
  • Communication: Send updates about issue status and platform news
  • Rewards System: Track points and manage reward redemptions

AI and Analytics

We use your data to:

  • Train AI models for duplicate detection and smart routing
  • Analyze trends in civic issues across different areas
  • Improve our categorization and prioritization algorithms
  • Generate insights for municipal planning and resource allocation

Legal Compliance

We may use your information to:

  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations
  • Respond to legal requests and court orders
  • Protect our rights and prevent fraud
  • Ensure platform security and integrity

Information Sharing and Disclosure

Municipal Authorities

We share issue reports with relevant municipal departments to:

  • Enable issue investigation and resolution
  • Provide necessary context and evidence
  • Facilitate communication between citizens and authorities
  • Support emergency response when needed

Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party services for:

  • Cloud storage and hosting
  • Analytics and monitoring
  • Customer support and communication
  • Payment processing (for premium features)

These providers are bound by strict confidentiality agreements.

Public Information

Some information may be publicly visible:

  • Issue descriptions and photos (without personal identifiers)
  • General location information (neighborhood level)
  • Issue status and resolution progress
  • Community leaderboard rankings (username only)

We Do Not Sell Your Data

Samadhan Kendra does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Security and Protection

Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit and at rest
  • Access Control: Strict access controls and authentication
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Backup: Secure backup and disaster recovery systems

Data Retention

We retain your information for:

  • Account Data: As long as your account is active
  • Issue Reports: 5 years for historical analysis
  • Media Content: 3 years after issue resolution
  • Analytics Data: 2 years in anonymized form

Data Breach Response

In case of a data breach, we will:

  • Notify affected users within 72 hours
  • Report to relevant authorities as required by law
  • Take immediate steps to contain and remediate the breach
  • Provide guidance on protective measures

Your Rights and Choices

Access and Control

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all personal information we hold about you
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Portability: Download your data in a portable format

Communication Preferences

You can control:

  • Email notifications and newsletters
  • SMS alerts and reminders
  • Push notifications (mobile app)
  • Marketing communications

Location and Media Controls

You can:

  • Disable location services at any time
  • Delete uploaded photos and videos
  • Choose what information to share publicly
  • Opt out of AI analysis features

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Types of Cookies

We use cookies for:

  • Essential: Login sessions and security
  • Functional: User preferences and settings
  • Analytics: Platform usage and performance
  • Marketing: Relevant content and offers

Third-Party Services

We may use services that set cookies:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Social media platforms for sharing features
  • Payment processors for transactions
  • Customer support tools

Cookie Management

You can:

  • Control cookies through browser settings
  • Use our cookie preference center
  • Opt out of non-essential cookies
  • Delete existing cookies

Children's Privacy

Age Requirements

Samadhan Kendra is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Parental Consent

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately. We will take steps to remove such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

Policy Updates

We may update this privacy policy from time to time to:

  • Reflect changes in our practices
  • Comply with new laws and regulations
  • Improve transparency and clarity
  • Address new features or services

Notification Process

When we make significant changes:

  • We will notify you via email
  • Post a notice on our website
  • Update the "Last updated" date
  • Provide a summary of key changes
