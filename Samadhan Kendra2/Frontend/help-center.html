Getting Started

What is Samadhan Kendra?

Samadhan Kendra is India's premier AI-powered civic issue resolution platform. It enables citizens to report and track civic issues in their communities while earning rewards for their contributions.

How do I create an account?

Click the "Sign Up" button in the navigation bar. Fill in your details including name, email, phone number, and city. Verify your email and start reporting issues!

Is Samadhan Kendra free to use?

Yes! Samadhan Kendra is completely free for all citizens. You can report issues, track progress, and earn rewards without any cost.

Which cities are supported?

We currently support major Indian cities including Mumbai, Delhi, Bangalore, Hyderabad, Chennai, Kolkata, Pune, Ahmedabad, Jaipur, and Lucknow. More cities are being added regularly.

How do I report an issue?

Follow these steps:

  1. Click "Report Issue" in the navigation
  2. Take photos/videos of the issue
  3. Allow GPS location access
  4. Select issue category and priority
  5. Fill in details and submit

Why is GPS location important?

GPS location helps municipal authorities:

  • Pinpoint the exact location of the issue
  • Route the problem to the correct department
  • Plan efficient work schedules
  • Track issue density in specific areas

What should I include in my report?

For the best results, include:

  • Clear photos/videos showing the issue
  • Detailed description of the problem
  • When you first noticed the issue
  • Any safety concerns or urgency
  • Landmarks or nearby locations

Tracking Issues

How do I track my reported issues?

You can track issues in several ways:

  • View all your issues in the "My Issues" tab on your profile
  • Click "Track" on any issue card in the main issues list
  • Use the unique Issue ID provided when you submit
  • Check email notifications for status updates

How long does it take to resolve issues?

Resolution time varies based on:

  • Issue complexity and severity
  • Department workload and resources
  • Weather conditions and accessibility
  • Required permits or approvals

Simple issues may be resolved in days, while complex infrastructure problems may take weeks or months.

Can I get updates on issue progress?

Yes! You'll receive updates through:

  • Email notifications (if provided)
  • In-app status changes
  • Progress timeline on the tracking page
  • Department comments and updates

Account Management

How do I update my profile information?

To update your profile:

  1. Go to your Profile page
  2. Click "Edit Profile" or go to Settings tab
  3. Modify the information you want to change
  4. Click "Save Changes"

Can I change my email address?

Email address changes require verification:

  • Contact our support team
  • Provide proof of identity
  • Verify the new email address
  • Update will be processed within 24-48 hours

How do I manage notifications?

Control your notifications in Settings:

  • Email notifications for status updates
  • SMS alerts for urgent issues
  • Push notifications for app users
  • Newsletter and promotional emails

What if I forget my password?

Reset your password easily:

  1. Go to the Login page
  2. Click "Forgot Password?"
  3. Enter your registered email address
  4. Check your email for reset instructions
  5. Create a new strong password

Troubleshooting

I can't upload photos/videos

Try these solutions:

  • Check file size (max 10MB per file)
  • Ensure file format is supported (JPG, PNG, MP4)
  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Try a different browser
  • Check your internet connection

GPS location is not working

Common GPS issues and solutions:

  • Ensure location services are enabled
  • Allow location access when prompted
  • Check if you're outdoors or near windows
  • Try refreshing the page
  • Use manual address entry as backup

I'm not receiving email notifications

Check these settings:

  • Verify your email address is correct
  • Check spam/junk folder
  • Ensure notifications are enabled in settings
  • Add our email to your contacts
  • Check your email provider's filtering

The app is slow or not responding

Performance optimization tips:

  • Clear browser cache and cookies
  • Close unnecessary browser tabs
  • Check your internet connection speed
  • Try using the mobile app instead
  • Contact support if issues persist
