Points System Details

Points Validity

Annual Validity: Points earned in the current year are valid until December 31st.

Carry Forward: Unused points carry over to the next year.

Special Events: Points from special events may have different validity periods.

Points Limits

Daily Cap: Maximum 100 points per day from regular activities.

Monthly Cap: Maximum 2,000 points per month from all sources.

Special Bonuses: Achievement bonuses don't count toward daily/monthly caps.

Points Transfer

Non-Transferable: Points cannot be transferred between accounts.

Family Accounts: Family members can pool points for larger rewards.

Gift Cards: Some rewards can be gifted to other users.

Points Forfeiture

Account Suspension: Points may be forfeited for policy violations.

Fraudulent Activity: Points earned through fraud will be deducted.

Account Deletion: Unused points are lost when account is deleted.

Frequently Asked Questions

How long do points take to appear in my account?

Points are typically credited immediately after completing an action. In some cases, it may take up to 24 hours for points to appear.

Can I earn points retroactively for past activities?

No, points are only awarded for activities performed after joining the rewards program. Past activities cannot be retroactively rewarded.

What happens if I don't have enough points for a reward?

You'll need to earn more points before redeeming that reward. You can continue participating in activities to accumulate the required points.

Can I cancel a reward redemption?

Digital rewards cannot be cancelled once redeemed. Physical rewards can be cancelled within 1 hour of redemption if not yet processed.

Do points expire if I don't use them?

Points earned in the current year expire on December 31st. However, unused points carry forward to the next year.

How can I check my points history?

You can view your complete points history in your profile under the "Rewards" tab, including all earned and redeemed points.
